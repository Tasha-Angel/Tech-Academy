Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll directly through our website by visiting the Courses page and clicking "Enroll Now" on your chosen course. If you need assistance, our admissions team is available to help via phone or email.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. We also offer payment plans for many of our programs to make education more accessible.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! Our career services team provides resume reviews, interview preparation, and connects students with our network of hiring partners. Many of our graduates secure positions before completing their programs.

Can I take courses online?

All of our courses are available online with live instructor support. Some programs also offer in-person options at our San Francisco campus.

What if I need to change my course schedule?

We understand that life happens! Contact our student support team to discuss flexible scheduling options that work for you.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

We offer several scholarships and financing options. Please contact our admissions team to learn about current opportunities and eligibility requirements.
