About BreakThrough Tech Academy

Transforming lives through technology education

Our Story

Founded in 2018, BreakThrough Tech Academy began with a simple mission: to make quality tech education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or experience level.

What started as a small coding bootcamp has grown into a comprehensive tech academy offering courses in web development, data science, cybersecurity, and more.

Today, we've helped over 5,000 students launch careers in tech through our innovative, hands-on programs.

Our Mission & Values

Accessible Education

Community Focus
